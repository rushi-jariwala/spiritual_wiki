    Chapter 15
    Timeless Tuning




Many individuals move through life in a state of distraction,
their attention scattered among various thoughts, desires, and
emotions. This preoccupation often leads to a lack of mindfulness,
causing them to miss their immediate environment's subtle
energies and dynamics. On physical, spiritual, and mental planes,
they remain absent-minded, failing to harness the potential of
conscious living.

In stark contrast, Gurudev exemplified heightened alertness; his
awareness spanned multiple dimensions of existence. He was like
a cosmic commando, fully engaged and responsive to the intricate
dynamics of the universe. His consciousness was not confined to
the present; it spanned time, granting him insight into the past
and foresight into the future. With this deep understanding of
people, destiny, and events, his awareness was a double-edged
sword. He bore the weight of precognition, including its darkest
revelations, and shouldered the responsibility of shielding his
devotees, offering them guidance and protection from the storms
of misfortune.
