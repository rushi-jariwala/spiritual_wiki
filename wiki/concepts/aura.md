# Aura

## Definition

Aura is the bioelectric energy field that encompasses all life forms. It is the visible radiation of *shakti* — the spiritual energy accumulated by the [[concepts/sukshma-sharir|spirit body]].

> The goal of the spirit body or *sukshma sharir*, is the attainment of energy that can be used when it exists purely in its non-physical incarnation. This energy is called *shakti* and is manifested as the 'aura'.
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.II, p.15

The aura acts as a protective shield: diseases first infect the aura before reaching the physical body; negative spirits cannot usually penetrate a powerful aura.

> The aura acts like a protective shield to the body. Most external diseases we contract, first infect the aura and then the body.
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IV, p.40

Most saints and deities are depicted with halos — a symbolic representation of their powerful auras. The wider the aura, the more spiritually enriched the person.

## Scientific Evidence — The Kirilian Experiment

The existence of aura was scientifically demonstrated by Semyon Kirilian, a Soviet scientist who developed a method of photographing the aura. In an Indo-Soviet experiment conducted at Rishikesh in the early 1980s, 60,000 people were photographed using Kirilian photography. Analysis revealed:

| Group | Aura depth |
|---|---|
| Corporate executives, traders, businessmen | Least — 0.5 inches |
| Farmers, gardeners | Better |
| Yogis and philanthropists | Deeper still |
| Saints and spiritual practitioners | Greatest — up to 12 inches |

The spectrum of aura depth ranges from half an inch to twelve inches.

## Aura as Cosmic Currency

> **Golden Rule:** Wealth across lifetimes is measured in the currency of *shakti*. You are only as rich as your aura and its potential.
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IV, p.49

The [[concepts/sukshma-sharir|spirit body]] exists between incarnations for periods far longer than a single physical lifetime — potentially hundreds or thousands of years. For this, it requires *shakti* for sustenance, movement between realms, and protection from more powerful spirits. The aura is the measure and expression of that energy.

> [!tip] The physical body's deepest purpose is not worldly accomplishment but the generation of *shakti* — the spirit body's fuel and currency.

## Depletion of Aura

Aura is depleted by transfer and expenditure of energy:

1. Forward-bending exercises and weight training use up energy.
2. Massages, especially when fingers and toes are jerked outward.
3. Attraction toward the opposite sex — energy transfer occurs.
4. Sexual interaction — one partner gains more than the other.
5. Handshakes with a more powerful aura — energy flows to the stronger field. (Hingori recommends *namaste* as insurance.)
6. Curses, when they manifest, drain the giver heavily.
7. Desires and emotional attachments — *moh* (bonding) depletes energy, e.g., a mother's attachment to her child.
8. Anger — consumes energy rapidly.
9. Hatred — sustained loss, as it persists over time.
10. All *vrittis* — perception, imagination, sleep, memories, knowledge, misunderstandings — consume energy.
11. Excessive talking depletes aura. See also [[concepts/citt-vritti-nirodh]].

## Contamination of Aura

Aura quality can be polluted through energy exchange with lower *guna* fields:

- Wearing recently-worn clothes of others.
- Sitting in seats or sleeping in beds used by people with a different guna mix (buses, taxis, hotel rooms).
- Swimming in pools shared with predominantly *tamasic* or *rajasic* people.
- Drinking from another person's glass or eating from their plate (*jhootha*) — saliva transfers qualities directly. Consuming the saliva of a spirit-infected person can transmit the spirit.
- Food is affected by the eyes and thoughts of the cook; food captures the cook's mental state. *Prasad* is therefore cooked by evolved individuals.

## Measuring Your Aura

A simple self-assessment: face both palms toward each other and bring them slowly closer, stopping when you feel a light vibration between them.

| Distance | Assessment |
|---|---|
| 20–24 inches or more | Well-developed; spiritually reasonably healthy |
| 10–12 inches | Underdeveloped |
| Less than 6–8 inches | Auric poverty — urgent attention needed |

Test at least three to four times, as aura varies at different times of day and across days. *(Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.X, p.117)*

## Enhancement of Aura

- Touching the feet of a genuine saint: energy transfers from the more powerful aura. A purely scientific interaction.
- Drinking water or liquid sipped by a saintly person: acquires some of their qualities and strengths.
- Copper *kadas* with silver joints, blessed by someone with a powerful aura, retain and circulate energy around the bangle, establishing a connection to the greater field. See [[stories/hingori-kada-nairobi]].

## Protecting and Expanding Aura

### Six Methods

**1. Mantra Vidya** — Chanting silently in a meditative pose with hands and legs crossed (so vibration stays contained within the body). Practitioners keep their *malas* in cloth bags to prevent energy leakage through the rosary. A glass of water placed nearby while chanting captures the radiating energy; drinking it afterward absorbs the residual charge.

**2. Purification of the Mind** — A shift in guna mix toward *sattva* creates the potential for the *kundalini* to rise to levels where a larger aura can be sustained. It is an indirect but foundational method. See [[concepts/gunas]].

**3. Seva** — Service to people, animals, and other life forms invites gratitude (*dua*), which transfers as blessings that enhance the aura. See [[practices/seva]].

**4. Spiritual Healing** — Magnetising pain away from a patient adds to the healer's aura. But the healer may absorb some negative energy in the process and must *arpan* it (surrender it) to a higher power — failing which the healer suffers it:

> I recall an incident in Mumbai, where a patient had blisters in his mouth and throat. Being influenced by his family, I willed away the negative energy relieving him of his condition. Since I did not *arpan* the negative energy to a higher power, I possibly absorbed some of it and my son and I ended up with blisters in our mouths, lasting a few days.
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.X, p.118

See also [[stories/giri-blister-healing]] — negative energy magnetised from a house manifested as a face-shaped blister on Giri's hand before dissolving.

**5. Greening Places** — Watering plants and greening places adds to the aura balance. The plant kingdom can emit and transfer some of its aura; the exchange is mutual. Gardeners are typically richer in aura than their employers.

**6. Accidental Aura Attainment** — Every location carries its own magnetic energy field. Places with relatively higher magnetic values include: religious sites, cowsheds, gyms, frequently used road crossings, pyramids, caves, forests. Homes where people worship regularly become magnetically more powerful than those that do not. Meditating in such places may allow absorption of the higher energy — but this must not be practiced mindlessly. Taking energy without permission may invite a reaction:

> I learned the golden rule that energy was better earned than stolen.
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.X, p.120

See [[stories/hingori-renuka-temple-attack]] for the full account of Hingori's punishment at Renuka and rescue by [[entities/devi-renuka]].

### Thursday Restrictions — Guruvar

Gurudev's followers observe weekly restrictions on **Thursdays** (*Guruvar* — Guru/Jupiter's day, when Jupiter's rays most dominate):

- No shampooing hair — the palm's aura scratches the head's aura when massaging the scalp
- No shaving or cutting of nails and hair
- No washing clothes — worn clothes absorb the wearer's bio-magnetic aura; washing disturbs it
- No non-vegetarian food or alcohol — consuming animal flesh absorbs the aura and qualities of a lower-evolutionary species; a *tamasic* practice clashing with Jupiter's *sattvic* rays
- No intimacy

The principle underlying all restrictions is the same: preserve the aura on the day it is most susceptible to Jupiter's influence.

### Outward Transmission of Aura

Beyond the [[#Depletion of Aura|eleven depletion causes]], aura is also lost through outward transmission:

- **The eyes as transmitters** — energy flows out through the eyes, positive or negative. White-coloured foods (milk, curd, rice) are most susceptible to absorbing this energy, including negative energy from other people or spirits. Gurudev's instruction: mix any pure white food with a coloured food or spice before consuming.
- **Telepathy, desire, attraction, intense affection** — each creates an outward transmission that depletes the aura balance.
- **Creating an energy field around an external object** — also falls in the outward category.

Saints prefer to internalise rather than externalise — many spiritual teachers are hesitant to share knowledge freely, sensing the outward cost. The evolved ones accept it as a rewarding sacrifice.

### Animal Aura

The aura of animals can benefit human beings:

- **Bear water** — Gurudev advised bathing Hingori's son (born with weak legs) beneath a bear, so water first fell on the bear then on the son's legs. Hingori could not arrange it but acknowledges the principle.
- **Crow curd** — A village woman from Visakhapatnam advised Hingori's mother to feed him curd first eaten by a crow, to cure his delayed speech. It worked.
- **Black dog** — Gurudev taught that a black dog absorbs much of the negative energy in a house.

The plant kingdom points to the same inter-species connectivity: plants that visually resemble human body parts tend to benefit those parts.

> "Why are walnuts that look like the brain good for it? Why are papayas, that look similar to the stomach good for the stomach? Why are almonds that are shaped like the eyes good for the eyes?"
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.X, p.124

### The Bluetooth Principle — Criticism, Praise, and Meditation

> When you think of someone or something, a remote bluetooth sort of connection is established. When you criticise someone and their qualities, even unwittingly, you magnetise a fraction of the qualities of the person. Therefore, criticising someone regularly will, at some point, instil within you the very qualities that you criticised.
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.X, p.125

Praising or admiring someone has the same connecting effect — the qualities of the praised are partially absorbed. From yogic practice: to acquire specific qualities, meditate on animals that embody them — a horse for speed, an elephant for strength, a lion for courage, a spider for patience.

> **Golden Rule:** What you meditate upon, that you become. Choose prudently.
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.X, p.125

> [!tip] This principle connects [[concepts/empathy]] (sustained attention to others) and [[concepts/pratipakshbhavana]] (redirecting the mind's focus). Consistent attention to a quality — in another person, an animal, or a divine figure — gradually encodes it in one's own field.

## Black Magic on Aura

Personal objects containing traces of an individual's aura (hair, nails, coins, clothes, shoes, handwritten notes) can be used by practitioners of negative *tantric vidya*, voodoo, or black magic to target and damage that person's aura. The potency of the attack depends on the relative strength of the attacker's and target's auras.

Notable cases:
- A child lost all sensation from the waist down after a practitioner used his stolen swimming trunks.
- Hingori himself contracted rheumatoid arthritis for several years from black magic transmitted through a *peda* (sweetmeat). Cured by Gurudev. See [[stories/hingori-arthritis-cure]].
- Hingori's security guard had black magic performed on a shirt Hingori left behind; the guard amusingly confessed the act himself. — *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IV, p.48
- Kamlesh was nearly killed by black magic — vomited glass bangles over months; fully cured when Gurudev extracted the embedded *taveez*. See [[stories/kamlesh-black-magic-cure]].
- A media personality found lemons pierced with needles in her cupboard; contracted fever; the *Mahagayatri Mantra* chanted for hours saved her within 24 hours.

> [!warning] Anyone's aura can be infiltrated by practitioners of black magic, provided they have access to the person's personal objects. (Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IV, p.48)

## Cosmic Currency — Aura in the Afterlife

Ch.XIV extends the aura-as-cosmic-currency teaching into its fullest statement:

> "Cosmic currency is basically earning energy that will be added to your own aura and *shakti*. It can be used for astral living and to attain *gati* in the afterlife. The spirit body needs this energy to exist, travel and protect itself. It can also attain this energy through deeds done in its name, either by itself or by others."
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.XIV, p.186

Charity done in the name of ancestors adds to *their* cosmic currency in the afterlife. The spirit body's post-death capacity for astral travel, protection from more powerful spirits, and eventual ascent through the *loks* all depend on the aura accumulated during physical life — and on any *seva* or charity performed in the spirit's name by living descendants. See [[concepts/loks]] for the lok-by-lok system; [[practices/seva]] for the mechanisms of seva credit.
