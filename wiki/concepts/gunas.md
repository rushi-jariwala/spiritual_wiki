# Gunas

> *Stub — introduced in passing in Ch.5 in the context of astral planes. Expanded treatment expected in later chapters / sources.*

## Definition

**Gunas** are fundamental qualities or tendencies of an entity. Three gunas are named:

- **Tamasic** — inertia, dullness, lower quality
- **Rajasic** — activity, passion, middle quality
- **Sattvic** — purity, clarity, higher quality

## In Ch.5 Context

Gunas are used to describe the work of spirits on higher astral planes who descend to help elevate entities on lower planes:

> Helping an entity with too much tamasic guna, reducing and replacing it with part sattvic and part rajasic gunas, help improve the entity's basic nature. Similarly, descending from the third level to the second, to help the inhabitants replace some rajasic qualities by more sattvic ones, help raise the bar. Most prophets who have taken birth on earth in a body form have had the same purpose in mind.
> — Hingori, *Karma Sutra*, Ch.5, p.84

The elevation of gunas — tamasic → rajasic → sattvic — is thus both an inner practice and a service performed by more evolved beings for less evolved ones, across astral planes and within embodied life.
