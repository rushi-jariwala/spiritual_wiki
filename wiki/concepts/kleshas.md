# Kleshas

> *The five stresses or confusions that affect the human mind. "Ignorance" (avidya) is one of them.*

## Status

**Stub.** Chapter 1 of *Karma Sutra* mentions the kleshas only in passing while defining the role of the guru. The book's table of contents shows "The Kleshas" as its own section in "Circle of Life After Life" — fuller treatment will be added when that section is ingested.

## What Chapter 1 Says

> Ignorance here refers to **avidya**, which is one of the five kleshas or stresses/confusions that affect the human mind.
> — *Karma Sutra*, Ch.1, p.19

This is the only mention. What we can infer:
- There are **five** kleshas
- They are best understood as **stresses** or **confusions** — not simply moral failings
- **Avidya** (ignorance) is one of them, and is the specific klesha that a guru addresses by definition (see [[concepts/guru-disciple]])
- They affect the human mind, implying they sit at a psychic/mental level rather than the bodily one
