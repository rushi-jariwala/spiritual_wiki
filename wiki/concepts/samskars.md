# Samskars

> *Stub — introduced in passing in Ch.5. Expanded treatment expected in later chapters / sources.*

## Definition

**Samskars** are the form in which accumulated karma is stored.

> The form of storage is called samskars.
> — Hingori, *Karma Sutra*, Ch.5, p.90

In the Hingori framework, karma does not float free — it is encoded as samskars, which then shape destiny. A person's [[concepts/sanchit-karma|sanchit]] is held as samskars; the [[concepts/mahurat|mahurat]] is calibrated to allow those samskars to fructify.
