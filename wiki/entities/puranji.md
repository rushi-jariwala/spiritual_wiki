# Puranji

## Who / What

**Puranji** (also referred to as Puran ji) is a **mighty saint** who was an important part of the Gurgaon sthan, where he spent over a decade serving people daily. Gurudev treated him like a son. He also served as Gurudev's **emissary** — appearing in Hingori's dream state to call him for an out-of-body tour that Gurudev had arranged. In the physical world at that moment, Puranji was present at the sthan massaging Gurudev's feet while Gurudev had astrally left his body.

> His emissary, Puranji, appeared in my dream state and said, "Gurudev is calling you."
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.VI, p.67

Buddhe Baba also appeared to Puran ji in a dream-state vision, with a physical description that matched the independent account of Roopal ji — the two corroborating each other (*The Guru of Gurus*, p.50).
