# Puranji

## Who / What

**Puranji** is Gurudev's **emissary** — a disciple who appeared in Hingori's dream state to call him for an out-of-body tour that Gurudev had arranged. In the physical world, Puranji was present at the *sthan* and was massaging Gurudev's feet while Gurudev had astrally left his body.

> His emissary, Puranji, appeared in my dream state and said, "Gurudev is calling you."
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.VI, p.67

> [!question] Full identity
> Beyond being Gurudev's emissary and a disciple present at the *sthan*, nothing further about Puranji is established from currently ingested sources.
