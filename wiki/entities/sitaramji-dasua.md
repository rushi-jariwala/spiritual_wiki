# Sitaramji of Dasua

## Who

Sitaramji of Dasua was Gurudev's spiritual advisor in the **early years, before the power of the Mahaguru awakened in him**. Gurudev consulted him frequently during that period.

Sitaramji of Dasua himself had a guru: **Sitaramji of Banaras**.

> "In his early years, before the power of the Mahaguru awakened in him, Gurudev had a spiritual advisor by the name of Sitaramji of Dasua, whom he often consulted. Sitaramji of Dasua had a guru called Sitaramji of Banaras. Eventually, Gurudev had a mentor he addressed as Buddhe Baba."
> — Hingori, *Guru Sutra*, Ch.2, p.12

## Significance

Sitaramji of Dasua represents the first link in the recorded spiritual lineage that preceded Gurudev's full awakening as a mahaguru. Gurudev himself — universally remembered as a spiritual giant — passed through a preparatory phase of seeking guidance. This underscores Hingori's thesis that no evolution, however exalted, bypasses the guru-disciple relationship.
