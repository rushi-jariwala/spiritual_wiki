## Pillars (to be developed)

- **Karma** — mechanics of cause and effect, karmic accounts
- **Inner anatomy** — kleshas, koshas, and gunas as the three-dimensional map of the human being; Ch.7 gives the first complete picture of what karma is *operating on*
- **Atma / Soul** — nature of the soul, its journey, unveiling
- **Guru-Disciple relationship** — role of the Guru, surrender, grace
- **Dream states** — dreams as spiritual experience and perception
- **Practices** — practical guidance for daily life and sadhana

## Synthesis (2026-04-15)

Two cross-cutting patterns identified after full ingestion of Karma Sutra. Filed as synthesis pages:

- [[synthesis/inner-frameworks-convergence|The Convergence of Inner Frameworks]] — Gunas, koshas, attitude-to-deeds, and non-doership all map the same three-stage inward movement toward witness-consciousness. Different lenses on one process.
- [[synthesis/samskar-loop|The Samskar Loop]] — The four-link mechanism by which each act generates the conditions for its own repetition. The technical break is non-doership at the citta level.
