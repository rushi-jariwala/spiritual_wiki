## On Aura as Protective Shield

> "The aura acts like a protective shield to the body. Most external diseases we contract, first infect the aura and then the body."
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IV, p.40

## On the Nature of the Spirit Body

> "The spirit body is made of ether, almost like a body made of smoke (one-tenth as dense as smoke) slightly lit from within. Because of its ultra-light weight and subtle vibration, it does not need to move slowly like its physical counterpart and it can fly at will."
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IV, p.42
[[concepts/sukshma-sharir]]

> "The evolved spirit body mainly observes dispassionately. Unevolved spirit bodies often remain stuck in their attachments and take time and multiple lives to get to higher levels of existence."
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IV, p.42

## On Aura and Awareness

> "All of us have an aura, a body of energy linked to different planes of awareness. Some have the ability to access more planes of awareness than others because of past lives, because of practice in this life."
> — Frederick Lenz, quoted in *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IV, p.44
