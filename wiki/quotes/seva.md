# Quotes on Seva and Service

> "*Seva* works in many ways. It is a precursor to self-esteem. Besides, it is also a demonstration to the inner bodies of the efforts made by the physical one to evolve itself."
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IX, p.112

> "*Seva* must be done along with the practice of non-doership."
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IX, p.113

> "**Golden Rule:** Never accept any return (monetary or otherwise) for your service, or the accrual of *seva* in lieu of the service, gets nullified."
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IX, p.113

> "*Seva* works like a multilevel marketing concept. There are benefits for those who do the *seva* directly, and for those who are indirect facilitators for the same."
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IX, p.113

> "Spiritual healing and helping people evolve is considered the highest form of *seva*."
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IX, p.114

> "Service to other life forms is service to the consciousness supreme and service to the self."
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IX, p.115

> "Of what value is charity performed with wealth earned by deceiving others? Only they derive the fruits of such charity!"
> — Guru Vashisht, *Yog Vasishta*, quoted in *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IX, p.115

> "A few minutes in every hour,
> A few hours in every day,
> Several days in every month,
> Every month in several years."
> That is the story of his *seva*.
> — Hingori on Gurudev, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IX, p.116
