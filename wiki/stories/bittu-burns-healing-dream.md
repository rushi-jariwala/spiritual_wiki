# Bittu Ji's Burns Healed Via Dream

## Full Narrative

> When Bittu ji sustained severe burns in a home fire, the mahaguru appeared in his dream, spoke to him, and offered him his sipped water. Within a week of this dream, Bittu ji's scorched skin flaked off, and he was able to walk.
> — *The Guru of Gurus*, p.228

## Source

*The Guru of Gurus*, Healing chapter, p.228. The story is recounted in the context of healing through dream and vision — conduits that Gurudev used both during his lifetime and after his passing.

An earlier account in the book established that the healing restored Bittu ji's vision following third-degree burns sustained in a house fire (p.221), making this one of the most dramatic healing cases in the tradition.
