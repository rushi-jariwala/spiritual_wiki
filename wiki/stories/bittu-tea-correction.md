# Gurudev Serves Tea for Three Days, Then Gives Bittu Ji an Earful

## Full Narrative

> For the first few days after they arrived at the camp at Renuka, Gurudev would wake Bittu ji at 3 am to serve him a hot cup of tea. Bittu ji would leave the cup unwashed and return to sleep after enjoying the early morning beverage brewed by his guru. For three days, Bittu ji revelled in the luxury of being served by his guru, but Gurudev gave him an earful on the fourth day. On being reprimanded for the dereliction of his duties, Bittu ji divorced his complacency. After that, he not only made the tea, but also washed the cups!
> — Hingori, *The Guru of Gurus*, p.83

## Source

*The Guru of Gurus*, "The Server, Not the Served," p.83
