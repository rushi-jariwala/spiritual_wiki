# Charan Singh Ji's Daughter — "He Is Shiv"

## Full Narrative

> During Charan Singh ji's tenure as head of the Radha Soami Satsang Beas, his daughter contracted cancer. He asked his wife and daughter to meet Gurudev, telling them, "He is Shiv. I treat him like a brother". When the mahaguru met with Charan Singh ji's daughter, he asked her to drink his sipped water. Disagreeing that she had cancer, he suggested that she undergo a medical re-examination to be doubly sure. The results indicated the tumour-like growth had receded.
> — *The Guru of Gurus*, p.229

## Source

*The Guru of Gurus*, Healing — Modes of Healing, p.229.

## Notes

The story is significant both as a healing account and as a testament to inter-lineage recognition. Charan Singh ji was at the time the head of the Radha Soami Satsang Beas — a major and independent spiritual tradition. His private acknowledgement of Gurudev as "Shiv" and his treating him "like a brother" speaks to a level of mutual recognition across traditions that was never publicly announced.

Gurudev's approach here was characteristically understated: he did not declare a cure but "disagreed" with the diagnosis and suggested the patient check again. The medical re-examination confirmed the change.
