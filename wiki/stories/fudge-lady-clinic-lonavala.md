# The Fudge Lady's Clinic — Karmic Investment in Lonavala

## Full Narrative

> Medical aid can help alleviate physical suffering of the needy. If one can help such people, one has the right to claim positive karma and in plenty. Philanthropists fund hospitals, provide free medical treatment, organise medical camps and free distribution of medicines. Several doctors work pro bono for poor patients.
> 
> A lady who was in the business of making fudge in Lonavala, near Mumbai, set up a homeopathic clinic there providing free treatment. After the lady's death, her friends carried on her good work and today hundreds of poor people get treated at this clinic. Her investment in a one-bedroom apartment has benefitted and will continue to benefit many more.

> "A better proposition of an investment-reward-ratio may not be found even on Wall Street!"
> — Hingori, *Karma Sutra*, p.65
