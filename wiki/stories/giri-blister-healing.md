# Giri's Blister — Negative Energy Made Visible

## Full Narrative

> One of my mother's friends was an interesting case study. She constantly complained of her family being under the influence of black magic, as they felt uneasy in their home. On her request, I decided to take my spiritual associates, Bipan and Giri, to sort out the issue. I asked my friend Giri to assess if there was any place in the house where he felt uneasy or his head felt heavy. Sure enough, Giri experienced heaviness and negativity in a part of the house. We used certain *mantras* to magnetise some water, which he then splashed in that specific area. He had to do that repeatedly as the negative energy was difficult to get rid of.
>
> What happened next was shocking. A huge blister appeared on Giri's hand just below his thumb. It had the shape of a face, like one outlined by a child. Shocked, Bipan and I watched as the blister was slowly absorbed in his hand, eventually disappearing within 15 minutes. Giri's hand magnetised the negative energy, thereby clearing it from the lady's house.
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.X, pp.121–122
