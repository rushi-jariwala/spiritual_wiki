# Giri's Embarrassing Dream — Gurudev Nips Guilt in the Bud

## Full Narrative

> Here is an interesting story of thoughts or dreams perceived as unacceptable. Giri, a devout follower of Gurudev, while in Gurgaon, had a sensual dream. Embarrassed about it, he went up to Gurudev and confessed. Gurudev was empathetic and asked him not to feel guilty. He said that these things would stop occurring shortly after. By saying so, he averted Giri's embarrassment from getting converted into a *samskar*.
>
> Another follower complained of having sensual dreams about his own mother. He was emotionally traumatised by such licentious dreams. Gurudev told him not to think of it anymore, and it would never happen again. And it did not. Such examples show by feeling guilty, the disciples would have formed low opinions of themselves. Gurudev did not want this to happen. And so, he nipped the guilt in the bud.
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.III, pp.32–33
