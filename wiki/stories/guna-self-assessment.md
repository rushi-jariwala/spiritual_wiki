# Guna Self-Assessment Worksheet

## Source

*Aatma Sutra*, Ch.VIII, pp.96–97

## Worksheet (Verbatim)

> Find out your dominant guna by rating yourself on the following attributes:
>
> | S No. | Attribute | Score 1 | Score 2 | Score 3 |
> |---|---|---|---|---|
> | 1 | Diet | Natural and vegetarian | Processed foods and meat in moderation | High intake of processed foods and meat |
> | 2 | Intoxicants | Never | Sometimes | Regularly |
> | 3 | Sleep | Little | Moderate | A lot |
> | 4 | Sensory Pleasures | Little | Moderate | A lot |
> | 5 | Speech | Calm | Forceful | A lot |
> | 6 | Cleanliness | High | Moderate | Low |
> | 7 | Work Ethic | Selfless | Driven | Lazy |
> | 8 | Anger | Rarely | Sometimes | Frequently |
> | 9 | Depressed | Never | Sometimes | Frequently |
> | 10 | Violence | Never | Sometimes | Frequently |
> | 11 | Attachment to Money | Low | Somewhat | A lot |
> | 12 | Willpower | Strong | Variable | Weak |
> | 13 | Practice of Giving | Frequent | Sometimes | Rarely |
> | 14 | Spiritual Study | Regularly | Sometimes | Rarely |
> | 15 | Meditation | Regularly | Occasionally | Rarely |
> | 16 | Contentment | High | Sometimes | Rarely |
> | 17 | Forgiveness | Mostly | Sometimes | Rarely |
> | 18 | Honesty | Always | Mostly | Rarely |
>
> Note your personal score for each attribute and add up the total.
>
> Note the score under each guna personality:
>
> Score of 18–27 = *Sattvic*
> Score of 28–36 = *Rajasic*
> Score of 37 or above = *Tamasic*
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.VIII, pp.96–97
