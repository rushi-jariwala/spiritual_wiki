# Guru Sutra Ch.2 — Opening Verse

Epigraph to *Guru Sutra* Ch.2, p.9. Attributed to **Guru Arjan Dass** — the fifth Sikh Guru (1563–1606).

---

> He who created this body, this house of clay,
> Has locked it
> And to the guru has entrusted the key.
> Without the guru the treasure within
> Is never gained,
> Even though you may try to gain it
> In endless ways.
> — Guru Arjan Dass

---

## Source

Hingori, *Guru Sutra*, Ch.2, p.9

## Related Concepts

- [[concepts/guru-disciple]] — the verse encapsulates the guru's role: only through the guru is the inner treasury accessible
- [[concepts/moksha]] — the "treasure within" is the jivaatma's inherent divinity; liberation is gaining what was always there
