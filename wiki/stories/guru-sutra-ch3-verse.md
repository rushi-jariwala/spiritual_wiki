# Guru Sutra Ch.3 Closing Verse — Unfettered Devotion

**Source:** Hingori, *Guru Sutra*, Ch.3, p.26

> Unfettered devotion is how
> spiritual journeys begin.
> They culminate with the realisation
> of the divinity within.

Closes Chapter 3 (The Gurukul System). The verse frames the entire gurukul arc — from the first stirring of surrender through the disciplined path of scriptures, mantra vidya, and mentorship — as a single movement toward self-realisation. The journey begins in bhakti and ends in the recognition that the divine was always within.
