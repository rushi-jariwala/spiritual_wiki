# "Let no man in this world live in delusion" — Guru Nanak Dev

Closing epigraph of *Guru Sutra* Ch.5, p.42.

> "Let no man in this world live in delusion.
> Without a Guru, none can cross over to the other shore."
>
> — Guru Nanak Dev

Placed immediately after Hingori's argument that spiritual hierarchy should be measured by attainment and not chronology. The verse anchors the entire chapter's case for the irreplaceability of the guru — whatever the disciple's stage, the guru remains the bridge.

## Source

*Guru Sutra*, Ch.5, p.42 — closing epigraph.
