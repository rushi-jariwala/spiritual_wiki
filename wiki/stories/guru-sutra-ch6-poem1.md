# Guru Sutra Ch.6 Poem — "By Blowing the Trumpet" (p.50)

Verbatim verse from *Guru Sutra* Ch.6, p.50. Appears between the sections on "Desire for Self-importance" and "Spiritual Vanity."

## Verse

> By blowing the trumpet to satiate pride,
> By allowing the littleness to grow tall,
> By befriending the chicanery of self-importance & praise,
> you've set the soul up for a mighty fall.
> — Hingori, *Guru Sutra*, Ch.6, p.50

## Source

*Guru Sutra*, Ch.6 — "Journey Towards Disciplehood," p.50
