# Guru Sutra Ch.6 Poem — "The Scales Will Tip" (p.58)

Verbatim verse from *Guru Sutra* Ch.6, p.58. Appears within the "Judging Oneself" section, as a counterpoint to harsh self-assessment.

## Verse

> The scales will tip one way or another.
> The sea will surge and the tide will ebb.
> Harsh self-judgement is a trap
> so steer clear of its treacherous web.
> — Hingori, *Guru Sutra*, Ch.6, p.58

## Source

*Guru Sutra*, Ch.6 — "Journey Towards Disciplehood," p.58

- [[concepts/guru-disciple]] — within the "Judging Oneself" section: the disciple must not evaluate themselves harshly or against others; spiritual growth is a tide, not a snapshot
