# Guru Sutra — Introduction Closing Poem

**Source:** Hingori, *Guru Sutra*, Introduction (closing verse)

---

> He lights the divine flame
> which you can nurture with devotion.
> Soon your every atom knows
> you're not a wave but the eternal ocean.

— Hingori

---

The verse closes the Introduction to *Guru Sutra*. The first two lines describe the guru's act — he initiates the flame; the disciple sustains it through devotion. The third and fourth lines name the destination: the dissolution of the wave-identity into the ocean-identity. "Every atom knows" points to a full-body, not merely intellectual, realisation.

The wave/ocean image recurs across the Hingori corpus as the primary analogy for jivaatma and aatma — the individual soul and the consciousness supreme. See [[concepts/aatma]], [[concepts/jivaatma]].
