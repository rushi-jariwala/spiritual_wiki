# The Himachal Pradesh Jeep Ride — Gurudev Exhausts Hingori's Vitarks

## Full Narrative

> I was travelling with Gurudev to one of his official camps in Himachal Pradesh. On the way, I confessed to him that I suffered from a lot of negative thoughts and asked for help. On hearing this he smiled wickedly. We got into the jeep and what a bumpy ride it was! I started having strange thoughts of Gurudev urinating, and of myself behaving badly with him. I got thoughts of being profane and worse, and the speed of these thoughts was phenomenal — one after the other, one worse than the other! It felt like a thousand years had passed by the time we reached our destination.
>
> Feeling awkward in Gurudev's presence, I sought his permission to go to bed straightaway. The next morning, I woke up early, my mind was quiet. I could feel the crispness of the air, smell the greenery and the soil. Was this peace of mind? It had to be. Unlike the previous evening, there were fewer thoughts and more observations. The *vitarks* had faded away, probably because of their over-exhaustion the evening before, providing much needed relief.
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.III, pp.34–35
