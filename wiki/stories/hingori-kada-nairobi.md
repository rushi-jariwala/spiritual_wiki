# Hingori's Kada in Nairobi

## Full Narrative

> I am embarrassed to share how my *kada* helped me during a visit to Nairobi over 30 years ago. For 20 days I tried to do something against my conscience. But each time something would go wrong. Logically, it is not possible to fail so many times. I knew there was something that was forcing me to follow the right path. The *kada* I wore was given by Gurudev, and every time I failed at attempted wrongdoing, the *kada* kept flashing in my mind. By the end of the month-long trip, I knew for sure that the *kada* had something to do with my failed attempts. Its energy had actually manipulated my mind.
