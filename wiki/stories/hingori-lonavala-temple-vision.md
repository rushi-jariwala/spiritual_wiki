# Hingori's Lonavala Temple Vision

## Full Narrative

> During the dream and vision states, I sometimes found myself travelling with Gurudev to different locations and everything seemed so real that I could feel the breeze and even touch things. Sometimes, the travels included visiting well-known temples I had never been to before. He took me to a temple in Lonavala, which was built by one of his young disciples. I saw the temple structure and the idols in colours of grey and black, not very common in that part of the country. The experience lasted for several minutes.
>
> A few days later, I came across Gurudev's disciple who had built a temple in Lonavala. He requested me to come and see the temple. On reaching, I was shocked to see the same temple that Gurudev had taken me to a few months ago, and it was exactly the way I had seen it in my dream.
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.VI, p.68
