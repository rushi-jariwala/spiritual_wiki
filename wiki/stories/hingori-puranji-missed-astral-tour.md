## Full Narrative

> My astral travel started with Gurudev telling me casually that he would take me for a long out-of-body tour. Gurudev asked me to go to sleep while he lay down doing *paath* (focused *mantra* practice), that was when he would travel astrally. While I was asleep, his emissary, Puranji, appeared in my dream state and said, "Gurudev is calling you". Limited by my physical orientation, I did not realise that I should take the help of the emissary, walk out of the body, and follow him. Instead, I forced myself to physically wake up and go to his room. There I found Puranji massaging Gurudev's feet whilst he had left his body. I missed my chance of astral travel that night.
