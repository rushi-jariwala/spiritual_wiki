# Hingori Sees a Thought

## Full Narrative

> I did not think so, until I saw a thought. This is not a misprint. I actually saw a thought! Back in the day, I used to meditate for hours through the night. During one such meditation, on opening my eyes, I saw a ray emerging from one end of the room and hitting a point a few inches above the centre of my forehead. It soon dispersed in my head and the thought was crystal clear. I now perfectly understood what Gurudev said. It was time to re-evaluate the subject.
>
> If most thoughts were external and merely received by the mind, then how was I responsible for them? A radio receiving several external signals is not responsible for the content of the signals it receives. It has no reason to feel proud or embarrassed. For me, this has been one of the greatest pearls of wisdom.
