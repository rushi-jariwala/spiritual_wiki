## Full Narrative

> 35 years ago was when Gurudev first opened my third eye. This blessed me with several experiences which I shared with him. What seemed cruel at that time, was that he scanned his hand from my head to my lower back and shut my third eye. I could have otherwise answered any question on any subject to anyone at any time. The reason he did this was because he did not want me to get stuck at the level of the third eye, enjoying the capabilities and amazements which would not allow me to move further. Mine was not a unique case, as he did this to most of his disciples, whom he helped qualify for even higher loks. He disallowed the usage of the third eye capability, lest they become obstacles to their progress.
