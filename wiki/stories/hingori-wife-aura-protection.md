# Hingori's Wife's Aura — The Blue Conical Light

## Full Narrative

> On one of my 'out-of-the-body' journeys, I saw my wife sleeping on the floor. It was around 4 am. My wacky sense of humour compelled me to pull her leg (no pun intended) by bouncing on her to scare her. What happened next was a great lesson. Every time I tried to jump on her, I would bounce right back. I was unable to touch her, as she was enveloped by a blue conical light, extending at least a foot off her body. She did not move an inch, despite my best efforts to wake her. That is when I realised that the human aura is a powerful protective shield.
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IV, p.39

*Aatma Sutra*, Ch.IV, "What is Aura", p.39.
