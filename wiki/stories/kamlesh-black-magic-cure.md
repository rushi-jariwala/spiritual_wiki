# Kamlesh's Black Magic Cure

## Full Narrative

> I witnessed a patient, Kamlesh, who stayed at the *sthan* for several months to get respite from black magic. She would experience excruciating pain in her stomach and writhe on the floor like a fish out of water, until she fainted. I saw her vomit broken pieces of glass bangles tied to a black thread, on several occasions. The disciple treating her, Mohna, would wash the glass pieces and stick them together on a piece of paper, forming a complete bangle. I was told a black magic practitioner had given her a glass of rose milk containing a *taveez* (locket), which held a piece of paper with her name written on it. Gurudev said that she would be completely cured once she vomited the *taveez*. I was around when she complained of choking to Gurudev. He hit her on the back a couple of times and the *taveez* popped out from her mouth. Kamlesh was finally cured. She now lives a normal life with her husband and children. The attempt to kill her was foiled.
