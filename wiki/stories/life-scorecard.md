# The Life Scorecard

**Source:** *Aatma Sutra*, Life Scorecard section, pp.189–196

## Purpose

> "The objective of this book is not to denigrate the quest of a normal householder's materialistic and intellectual pursuits. The life scorecard given below gives a balance to the average householder's lifestyle, giving equal weightage to the three main parameters of his life i.e. — physical, intellectual and spiritual."
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, p.190

Total marks: 300 (100 per part). Rating scale: 1 = haven't begun; 2 = quite far off; 3 = halfway; 4 = getting there; 5 = that's me spot on.

Gurudev would score a perfect 300. A score of 50 is considered good.

## Example Profiles

**Mr. Iyengar** — DGM (Finance), 55, own house, son's down payment covered, daughter married to a software engineer in the USA, well versed in Hindu scriptures, daily temple offerings, chants mantras 2 hours daily, guiding force in his community. → High on material; average on intellectual; **average on spiritual** (temple ritual = rajasic; mantra practice alone does not make a spiritual score high in Hingori's framework).

**Mr. Kapur** — Highly successful businessman; plush apartment; flat for son; 12–14 hours work daily; single malt at club; international calls at dinner; no philosophy; television over books; tries to arrange daughter's marriage against her wishes. → High on material/financial; **very low on intellectual and spiritual**.

## Notes on the Scoring System

> 1. A person scoring high on the spiritual front would not value his score in the other two attributes, simply because he would have learned that his life is not limited to the 70 odd years he has to spend in this body.
> 2. Under the spiritual accomplishments heading, we have tried to create a benchmark... Although spiritualism is not a numbers' game, we have tried to create such a system for non-spiritual individuals.
> 3. Under our benchmarking system, a person like Gurudev would have easily got a perfect score.
> — Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, pp.190–191

## Full Scorecard (verbatim)

### PART A — PHYSICAL

**A1 Material** (rate 1–5 each):
- I am successful at work.
- I have a beautiful home.
- I have a fancy car.
- I am well-traveled.
- I am well dressed.

**A2 Financial** (rate 1–5 each):
- I have a good bank balance.
- I have a regular source of income.
- My investments will see me through the rest of my life.
- I have plenty of assets.
- I will leave behind enough wealth for my children.

**A3 Educational** (rate 1–5 each):
- I have high educational qualifications.
- I studied in the best of schools and colleges.
- I have always been high up in my class.
- I am academically inclined.
- My children are well-educated.

**A4 Social Achievements** (rate 1–5 each):
- I have successful family relationships.
- I have a group of close friends.
- I have a wide range of hobbies and interests.
- I belong to several social organisations.
- I am well respected socially.

**GRAND TOTAL _______ of 100**

---

### PART B — INTELLECTUAL

**B1 Philosophy** (rate 1–5 each):
- I am well-read.
- I have a high level of comprehension.
- I am fascinated with the life and thoughts of philosophers.
- I spend a lot of time on the study of philosophy.
- I have a deep understanding of philosophy.
- I can discuss philosophical concepts.

**B2 Awareness** (rate 1–5 each):
- I can sense the dynamics in a room the moment I enter it.
- All the decisions that I take by my intuition usually turn out right.
- I am well versed in subjects like astrology, vaastu, numerology, fengshui and other spiritual topics.
- I am able to anticipate events ahead of time.
- I have premonitions in my dreams.
- I have knowledge of the ancient scriptures.

**B3 Wisdom** (rate 1–5 each):
- I understand human nature.
- I live a life of simplicity and moderation.
- I am able to engage persons from all backgrounds and ages.
- I have a desire for *aatmic gyan*.
- I have the ability to amalgamate all in guiding people.
- I can control my sense cravings.
- I can empathise with other people.
- I can control my emotions.

**MY SCORE _______ of 100**

---

### PART C — SPIRITUAL

**C1 Philanthropy** (rate 1–5 each):
- I am passionate about philanthropy.
- I devote a reasonable percentage of my earnings to philanthropy.
- I support several charities or causes.
- I spend a lot of my personal time on philanthropy.

**C2 Service** (rate 1–5 each):
- I spend a fair share of my time serving others.
- I have served many people in my lifetime pro bono.
- I indulge in good karma.
- I put the needs of others ahead of those of my family.
- I consider myself fortunate for getting opportunities to serve people.

**C3 Mantra Vidya** (rate 1–5 each):
- I practice mantra vidya for more than an hour a day.
- I have been initiated into siddh mantras.
- I have attained more than one mantra siddhi.
- I have experienced the power of mantras.

**C4 Spiritual Accomplishments** (rate 1–5 each):
- I have the experience of the inner self.
- I have the power to heal others.
- I have had out-of-body experiences.
- I have had several spiritual experiences.
- I have attained the power of intuition.
- I believe all beings at the *aatmic* level are worthy of worship including myself.

**GRAND TOTAL _______ of 100**
