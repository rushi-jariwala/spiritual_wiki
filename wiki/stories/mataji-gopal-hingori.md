# Mataji's Psychic Reading — Gopal and Hingori

## Full Narrative

> When I was in college, I tried pursuing a girl unsuccessfully. I used every trick in the book, but to no avail. Disheartened, I visited a lady known for her psychic abilities called, 'Mataji', with my friend Gopal. Almost as soon as our session began, she warned Gopal that he would have to spend a fortnight in bed if he did not change his high-heeled boots. She also mentioned that he had an affair with a married Muslim lady, who lived in his neighbourhood. When he protested, she disclosed her name and described her appearance. Gopal was shocked at the accurate revelations and left in a hurry. He tripped a few days later, got a nasty sprain, and was bedridden for two weeks!
>
> I grew nervous and speechless when she asked me for the girl's photograph. It was a group photograph and there were other girls in it too, but she pointed out the one I was involved with. Looking at the photograph, she said there was no future in the relationship, and also that her mother had asthma and ulcers. The next day when I met the girl and asked her about her mother's ailments, she was surprised how I knew. And, yes, the relationship never took off. I thank my stars, as I eventually married a far nicer person (a little aggressive in old age, but nicer all the same).

— Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.V, pp.57–58
