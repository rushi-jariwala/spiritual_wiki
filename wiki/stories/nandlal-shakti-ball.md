## Full Narrative

> Nandlal, a regular visitor and *sevadaar* at the *sthan* for several years, was finding it difficult to rent out one of his several shops. Something would invariably go wrong just before finalising an agreement. He wanted our help as he felt the problem was spiritual in nature. As I was new to spiritual healing, I sat in meditation and requested Gurudev to give Nandlal the power to solve his problem. During meditation, I saw Nandlal, Gurudev and myself. Gurudev threw a ball of energy at me. It was dynamic, translucent white, almost a foot in diameter and felt alive. It kept expanding and contracting like a small imploding cloud.
>
> I threw this ball at Nandlal and it got absorbed within him. This power helped him clear his shop of the negative energies that were hindering his progress. A few days later, Nandlal met someone on the train who was in pain. He touched him and the pain vanished. Nandlal had become powerful enough to heal, though unaware of it.
>
> Another interesting incident in Nandlal's spiritual journey was when he egoistically splashed energised water into a patient's eyes to heal her (one of the processes taught by Gurudev). The eyes act like magnets that can receive and transmit energy. Unfortunately for Nandlal, the lady was spiritually advanced herself, with years of practicing powerful *mantras*, and because he was egoistic, and did not do this on behalf of the power of the *sthan*, Nandlal's hand got drained of energy. The energy was transferred, as the receiver of the energy was more powerful than the transmitter. It was a mistake he paid for heavily. For six months his right hand was weaker, suffering from an imbalance of aura.
