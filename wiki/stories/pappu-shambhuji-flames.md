## Full Narrative

> My friend, Pappu Sharma, lives in Jwalaji, Himachal Pradesh, and is a devotee of Gurudev. Like his father, Shambhuji, he is very spiritually evolved. Shambhuji, who died in 1980, was a respected man in his hamlet, and had been given spiritual powers by Gurudev. One day, while on an out-of-town visit, he was resting in a hut when Pappu saw balls of fire fall from the ceiling and extinguish before hitting his bed. He was shocked. Amidst the horrifying chaos, he saw his late father standing in the room protecting him from the flames. In a few seconds the flames disappeared and so did his father.
>
> In certain villages of India, the practice of black magic is widely prevalent. A dangerous trick in black magic is to send flames through an aerial route to burn someone's home. In the local language they call it *mutth chalana*. It was a learning experience for my friend who had never witnessed black magic before. Luckily, he was a gratified observer, who realised thereafter that he was spiritually protected.
