# Sabina's Tryst with a Psychic — The Fish Tank

## Full Narrative

> A well-known restaurateur was to launch a high-profile restaurant in Mumbai. The restaurateur's girlfriend, Sabina, went to see Ann Walker, a psychic in London, to discuss the fate of the venture. The psychic said it would do well and as an aside mentioned that she saw a small pond with fish in the premises. Sabina disagreed since there was no fish tank in the restaurant. But the psychic was certain of her prediction. When Sabina returned to Mumbai and visited the restaurant, she was surprised to see a fish tank. While she was away in London, her boyfriend had got it installed on the advice of their *vaastu* consultant. Her boyfriend (who is now her husband) is one of India's best-known restaurateurs.

— Hingori, *Aatma Sutra*, Ch.V, p.58–59
