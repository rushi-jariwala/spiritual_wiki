## Context and Use

Hingori quotes Tukaram immediately after prescribing Exercise 1 (photograph worship) and the "nine billion doorways" teaching. The verse provides the classical Marathi devotional grounding for Hingori's argument: **the image is a conduit, not the destination**. The worship passes through the earthen/stone/pewter medium and reaches the divine; the medium remains unchanged. Similarly, the photograph of yourself is not the object of worship â€” it is the conduit pipe between the physical appearance and the consciousness supreme at the other end.
