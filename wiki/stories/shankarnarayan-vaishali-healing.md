# Dr. Shankarnarayan and the Healing of Vaishali

## Full Narrative

> Dr Shankarnarayan, a senior colleague, was another person whose life was dramatically altered after meeting Gurudev. His family was dealing with a slew of problems. The most serious concern was the health of his young daughter, Vaishali, who suffered from intermittent fits and a persistent fever. When Shankarnarayan ji mentioned her failing health to Gurudev, the mahaguru smiled but said nothing.
>
> Over time, Shankarnarayan ji's faith in Gurudev's spiritual powers grew. His implicit faith in the man he saw as no ordinary healer led him to throw away his daughter's medicines, confident that she no longer needed them. Following that, Vaishali's condition gradually improved until she recovered completely. Dr Shankarnarayan was the first to address the man who had healed his daughter as 'Guruji'. Soon after, Gurudev initiated him as a disciple.
> — Hingori, *The Guru of Gurus*, pp.67–68
