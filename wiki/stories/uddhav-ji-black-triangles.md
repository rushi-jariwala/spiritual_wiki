# Uddhav Ji and the Black Triangles

## Full Narrative

> When Uddhav ji saw two black triangles with yellow eyes float into his room, he grabbed his nunchaku to lash out at them. Gurudev later informed him that he had to appease the accompanying triangular deity so that Uddhav ji would not have to pay the price for the nunchaku aggression with his life.
> — Hingori, *The Guru of Gurus*, pp.127–128

## Source

*The Guru of Gurus*, Entrepreneur Extraordinaire — People Management, p.127–128

## Context

Gurudev routinely tested and monitored disciples in disguise — appearing to them in forms they did not recognise, or sending attendants ahead of himself. Uddhav ji's instinct to attack the triangular apparition nearly cost him his life; Gurudev intervened astrally to pacify the deity accompanying the apparition. The episode illustrates both Gurudev's constant watchful presence across distance and the danger of reacting to the supernormal from ordinary reflexes.
